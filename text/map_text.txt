<u>Geo Analysis</u>
<br>
Comparison of food prices between states in India. 
Each Circle represents a single state (click them to see which).
<br><br>
Both the color and the size of a circle represent the price of the selected commodity. 
The duplication is intentional, and meant to help distuinguish and grasp the geographical trend more easily.
<br><br>
As one might expect, blocs of states within different geographical areas tend to have somewhat correlated prices.
